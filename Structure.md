# 6. On-brand meter (Design Scan Report)

Tool om designs automatisch te scannen op merkconsistentie.

### Checks performed
- **Logo usage** – correct color, allowed backgrounds  
- **Color** – approved token use  
- **Spacings** – margins & paddings volgens guidelines  
- **Typography styles** – italics vermijden, juiste hiërarchie  
- **Emotional resonance** – tone of image vs. brand  
- **Imagery** – juiste stijl, ambassador gebruik  
- **Copywriting** – actief, helder, to-the-point  
- **Brand asset usage** – gebruik van confetti, checkmark, etc.  
- **Accessibility** – WCAG 2.3 alignment  

### Example warning  
**Logo on unapproved color — Needs attention**  
Logo moet altijd op brand red (#E30027) staan.

### Output  
- Full report  
- Save report  
- Upload new design  
- Change specs

# 1. Introduction

## 1.1 Purpose of this document  
This document is a practical guide to help you create impactful content across all digital channels of the Postcode Lottery Group.

It is intended for writers and creators producing content for:
- The website  
- The mobile app  
- My Account pages  
- Newsletters  
- Press releases  

## 1.2 What this guide enables  
- Content that resonates with audiences  
- Clear and consistent messaging  
- A unified tone of voice across markets  
- Practical templates for core content types  
- Brand-safe and transparent communication  

## 1.3 How to use this document  

### If content creation is new to you:
1. Start with the Fundamentals.  
2. Jump to the relevant Content Guide.  
3. Follow the templates and structures.

### If content creation is part of your daily job:
1. Refresh the Fundamentals when useful.  
2. Navigate directly to the applicable content guide.  
3. Reuse the frameworks and examples.

## 1.4 Relationship to other brand documents  
- **Brand Book** – brand purpose and foundations  
- **Brand Playbook** – brand behavior and storytelling  
- **Content Playbook** – strategic content principles  
- **Digital Content Guidelines** – this document  
- **Website & App Manual** – channel-specific frameworks

# 2. Fundamentals

## 2.1 Tone of Voice  
Our tone of voice reflects who we are: clear, optimistic and human.

### Principles  
- **Straightforward** – simple and practical  
- **Optimistic** – warm and positive  
- **Inclusive** – easy to understand for everyone  
- **Practical** – focused on clarity and usefulness  

## 2.2 Structural Principles  

### Headlines  
- Short and active  
- Clear about what follows  

### Intro text  
- Sets context  
- Helps the user understand what they'll learn or do  

### Body copy  
- Short paragraphs  
- Use bullet points when helpful  
- Avoid unnecessary detail or complexity  

### Calls-to-action  
- Clear  
- Benefit-driven  
- Transparent about what happens next

# 3. Content Guides

These guides define structures and best practices for the most important content types.

---

## 3.1 How It Works  

### Objective  
Explain how the lottery works in a simple, transparent, and trustworthy way.

### Structure  
1. Headline  
2. Intro  
3. Key steps  
4. Benefits  
5. CTA  

### Principles  
- Avoid jargon  
- Use everyday language  
- Focus on clarity  
- Provide reassurance through transparency  

---

## 3.2 Charities  

### Objective  
Help users understand how contributions support good causes.

### Structure  
1. Charity name  
2. What they do  
3. Why it matters  
4. Real impact example  
5. CTA  

### Do  
- Use real stories and impact  
- Keep it authentic and respectful  

### Don’t  
- Overclaim impact  
- Use overly dramatic wording  

---

## 3.3 Winners  

### Objective  
Celebrate winner stories with joy and respect.

### Structure  
1. Context  
2. Winner(s)  
3. Prize amount  
4. Reaction / detail  
5. Visual  

### Core principles  
- Clear link to the Postcode Lottery  
- Include a positive, uplifting detail  
- Provide a transparent explanation of the prize  

---

## 3.4 Marketing Content  

### Objective  
Communicate benefits clearly to motivate users.

### Core elements  
1. Clear user benefit  
2. Transparent explanation of the mechanism  
3. Strong CTA  

### Structure  
1. Heading  
2. Benefit  
3. Explanation  
4. Bullet list  
5. CTA  

### Guidelines  
- Focus on value  
- Keep the language simple  
- Avoid pressure or excessive persuasion  

---

## 3.5 Plus & Premium  

### Objective  
Explain the extra value of premium tiers.

### Principles  
- Benefit-first  
- Clear differentiation  
- Short and easy to scan
